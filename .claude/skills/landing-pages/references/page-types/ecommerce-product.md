# E-commerce Product Landing Pages

Patterns for product pages that drive immediate purchases.

## Purpose

Convert visitors into buyers by showcasing product value, building trust through reviews, and reducing purchase friction.

## Key Differences from Other Pages

- Immediate purchase intent
- Price prominently displayed
- Product visuals are critical
- Reviews/ratings essential
- Urgency tactics effective
- Add to cart is primary action

---

## Page Structure

### Recommended Section Order

1. **Product Hero** - Images, price, add to cart
2. **Key Benefits** - Why buy this product
3. **Social Proof** - Reviews and ratings
4. **Features/Details** - Specifications
5. **Comparison** - vs. alternatives (optional)
6. **Guarantee** - Risk reducers
7. **Urgency** - Scarcity/time elements
8. **Related Products** - Cross-sell (optional)
9. **FAQ** - Product questions

---

## Product Hero Section

### Layout

```
[Breadcrumb: Home > Category > Product]

[Product Images]        [Product Info]
[Main Image]            [Product Name]
[Thumbnails]            [Star Rating] [Review Count]

                        [Price] [Compare price strikethrough]
                        [Variant selectors: Size, Color]

                        [Quantity selector]
                        [ADD TO CART - Large button]
                        [Buy Now / PayPal / Apple Pay]

                        [Key benefits - 3-4 bullets]
                        [Shipping info]
                        [Returns info]
```

### Product Images

**Requirements:**
- High-quality, zoomable images
- Multiple angles (4-8 images minimum)
- Lifestyle shots (product in use)
- Size/scale reference
- Video if applicable

**Order:**
1. Hero shot (main product view)
2. Alternate angles
3. Detail shots
4. Lifestyle/in-use shots
5. Size comparison (if relevant)

### Price Display

```
$99.99  $149.99 (33% off)
[Affirm/Klarna payment option: "or 4 payments of $25"]
```

**Price elements:**
- Current price (prominent)
- Original price (strikethrough if on sale)
- Discount percentage/amount
- Payment plan options
- Savings messaging

### Add to Cart Area

**Primary CTA:** "Add to Cart" or "Add to Bag"
**Alternative:** "Buy Now" (skip cart, go to checkout)
**Express checkout:** Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal

**Near CTA:**
- Free shipping threshold
- Returns policy summary
- Estimated delivery date

## E-commerce Copy Tips

### Product Title

Clear, searchable, descriptive:
> "[Brand] [Product Type] - [Key Feature/Variant]"
> "Lululemon Align Leggings - High Rise, 25" Inseam"

### Short Description

3-4 bullet points above the fold:
- Primary benefit
- Key feature
- Material/quality indicator
- Use case or audience

### Long Description

Expand below:
- Story/context
- Detailed benefits
- Use cases
- Care instructions

### CTA Copy

| Action | Copy |
|--------|------|
| Add to cart | "Add to Cart" / "Add to Bag" |
| Quick buy | "Buy Now" |
| Pre-order | "Pre-Order Now" |
| Out of stock | "Notify Me When Available" |
| Waitlist | "Join Waitlist" |

## Mobile Optimization

60%+ of e-commerce traffic is mobile.

**Mobile essentials:**
- Sticky "Add to Cart" button
- Swipeable image gallery
- Collapsible sections
- Large tap targets
- Quick payment options (Apple Pay)
- Clear variant selectors

---

## Common Mistakes

### Poor Product Images
Low quality, limited angles, no lifestyle shots.

### Hidden Price
Price should be visible without scrolling.

### No Reviews
Products without reviews convert significantly worse.

### Friction-Heavy Checkout
Guest checkout, multiple payment options, clear shipping.

### Missing Trust Signals
Guarantee, secure checkout, return policy must be clear.

### No Urgency (When Appropriate)
Low stock, limited time offers drive action.
