# Event Registration Landing Pages

Patterns for webinars, conferences, and time-based event registration pages.

## Purpose

Drive registrations for time-bound events by communicating value, establishing speaker credibility, and creating urgency around limited availability.

## Event Types

| Type | Typical Elements | Registration Style |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Webinar** | Single speaker, focused topic | Simple form |
| **Workshop** | Hands-on, interactive | Form + requirements |
| **Conference** | Multi-day, multiple speakers | Complex registration |
| **Masterclass** | Expert-led, premium | Application or payment |

---

## Page Structure

### Recommended Section Order

1. **Hero** - Event title, date/time, CTA
2. **Value Proposition** - What they'll learn
3. **Speaker/Host** - Credibility section
4. **Agenda** - What's covered
5. **Social Proof** - Past attendees, testimonials
6. **Urgency** - Limited spots, countdown
7. **FAQ** - Event logistics
8. **Final CTA** - Registration form

## FAQ Section

Answer logistical questions.

### Common Event FAQs

**About the event:**
- Is this event free?
- Will there be a recording?
- How long is the event?
- Is this live or pre-recorded?

**Logistics:**
- How do I join?
- What if I can't make the live time?
- Will I get reminders?
- What platform do you use?

**Content:**
- Is this for beginners or advanced?
- Will there be Q&A?
- What should I prepare in advance?

## Event Copy Tips

### Headline Formulas

**Outcome-focused:**
> "How to [Achieve Outcome] in [Timeframe]"

**Problem-solution:**
> "Stop [Pain Point]: A Live Workshop"

**Exclusive/insider:**
> "The [Secret/Strategy] Behind [Result]"

**Social proof:**
> "How [Expert] [Achieved Result] (And How You Can Too)"

### CTA Variations

| Context | CTA Copy |
|---------|----------|
| Free webinar | "Register Free" |
| Limited spots | "Save Your Spot" |
| Paid event | "Get Your Ticket" |
| Recording | "Get Access" |
| Waitlist | "Join the Waitlist" |

### Urgency Copy

- "Limited to [X] attendees"
- "Registration closes [date]"
- "[X] spots remaining"
- "Register by [date] for bonus"

## Common Mistakes

### Weak Value Proposition
"Join our webinar" doesn't convert. What will they LEARN?

### No Speaker Credibility
Why should they listen to this person? Establish expertise.

### Missing Date/Time
Date, time, and timezone must be immediately visible.

### Hidden Registration Form
Form should be visible above the fold.

### No Urgency
Limited spots, countdown, or bonuses create motivation.

### No Recording Option
Some can't attend live. "Register for the recording" captures more leads.
