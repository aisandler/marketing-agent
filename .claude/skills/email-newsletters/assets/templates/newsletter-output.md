## Final Checklist

Before sending:

- [ ] Subject line is compelling and different from recent issues
- [ ] Preview text extends (not repeats) subject
- [ ] Intro hooks attention immediately
- [ ] All sections complete and reviewed
- [ ] Links tested and working
- [ ] CTAs are clear and actionable
- [ ] Personalization tokens have fallbacks
- [ ] Mobile preview looks good
- [ ] Unsubscribe link is present and prominent
- [ ] Tone is consistent throughout
- [ ] Content matches what was promised in subject
- [ ] Send time is optimal for audience
