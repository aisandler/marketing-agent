## 3. Guide (Your Brand)

### Empathy
*Show you understand their problem*
- "We understand how frustrating it is when..."
- "We know what it's like to..."

### Authority
*Demonstrate your competence*
- Years of experience: [X years]
- Success metrics: [Statistics, testimonials]
- Credentials: [Certifications, awards]
- Social proof: [Reviews, case studies]

## 4. Plan (The Path)

### Process Plan
*How you solve their problem*
1. [Step 1 - Simple action]
2. [Step 2 - Clear process]
3. [Step 3 - Desired outcome]

### Agreement Plan
*Reduce their risk/fear*
- Guarantee: [What you promise]
- Terms: [How you protect them]
- Values: [What you stand for]

## 5. Call to Action

### Direct CTA
*Primary action you want them to take*
- [Schedule consultation]
- [Get quote]
- [Buy now]

### Transitional CTA
*Smaller commitment to build trust*
- [Download guide]
- [Free assessment]
- [Email series]
