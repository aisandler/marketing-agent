## Template Guidelines

### Timing Recommendations
- **Welcome Series:** 0, 3, 7, 14, 30 days
- **Lead Nurture:** 0, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30 days
- **Reactivation:** 0, 7, 14, 30 days
- **Seasonal:** -30, -14, -7 days from season

### Personalization Variables
```
{{CLIENT_NAME}} - Company name
{{FIRST_NAME}} - Subscriber first name
{{LOCATION}} - Subscriber city/area
{{NEARBY_LOCATION}} - Similar area for social proof
{{SERVICE_STATES}} - Service area states
{{PHONE_NUMBER}} - Company phone
{{WEBSITE_URL}} - Company website
{{YEARS_EXPERIENCE}} - Years in business
{{INDUSTRY_TYPE}} - Industry descriptor
{{BRAND_VOICE}} - Brand voice attributes
```

### Content Guidelines
- **Length:** 100-200 words per email
- **Tone:** {{BRAND_VOICE}} - helpful and educational
- **Focus:** Value-first, not sales-heavy
- **Mobile:** Optimized for mobile reading
- **Compliance:** CAN-SPAM compliant footers

### A/B Testing Opportunities
- Send day/time optimization
- Subject line variations
- Content length (brief vs. detailed)
- Personal vs. professional tone
- Image inclusion vs. text-only

### Performance Metrics
- **Open Rate Progression:** 40% ‚Üí 35% ‚Üí 30% ‚Üí 25% ‚Üí 20%
- **Click Rate Target:** 5-10% per email
- **Conversion Rate:** 15-25% by sequence end
- **Unsubscribe Rate:** <2% total sequence

### Automation Triggers
- **Welcome Series:** New customer signup
- **Lead Nurture:** Lead magnet download or contact form
- **Reactivation:** 90+ days since last engagement
- **Seasonal:** Calendar-based triggers
