**High-Urgency Pattern:** [Offer] + [Timeframe] + [Benefit]
**Examples:**
- "Save 20% on Fall {{INDUSTRY_TYPE}} Services - Limited Time"
- "{{LOCATION}} Special: Free Inspection + 15% Off Treatment"
- "Last Chance: Summer Service Package Ends Friday"

**Value-Focused Pattern:** [Benefit] + [Service] + [Location]
**Examples:**
- "Protect Your {{LOCATION}} Home - Special Service Package"
- "Early Bird Discount: Spring {{INDUSTRY_TYPE}} Protection"
- "Exclusive Offer for {{LOCATION}} Homeowners"

## Template Variations

### Seasonal Service Promotion
```
Subject: "Fall Prevention Special - Book Before Winter Weather Hits"

Focus: Prepare for seasonal challenges
Timeline: 2-4 week booking window
Urgency: Weather/seasonal deadlines
```

### New Customer Acquisition
```
Subject: "Welcome to the Neighborhood - 25% Off First Service"

Focus: Introduce services to new prospects
Timeline: 30-day offer window
Urgency: Welcome period expires
```

### Past Customer Reactivation
```
Subject: "We Miss You! Come Back Offer - 30% Off Return Service"

Focus: Win back dormant customers
Timeline: 3-6 months since last service
Urgency: Limited-time reunion offer
```

### Emergency/Problem-Focused
```
Subject: "Spotted [Problem Signs]? Fast Response Available"

Focus: Immediate problem resolution
Timeline: 24-48 hour response
Urgency: Problem escalation risk
```

## Content Guidelines

### Length Targets
- **Total Email:** 200-300 words
- **Subject Line:** 30-50 characters
- **Preview Text:** 35-90 characters

### Voice & Tone
- {{BRAND_VOICE}} - customer-focused
- Urgent but not pushy
- Value-oriented messaging
- Clear, direct communication

### Visual Elements
- Bold offer callouts
- Bullet points for benefits
- Contrasting CTA buttons
- Clean, mobile-friendly layout

## A/B Testing Elements

### Subject Lines
- Discount percentage vs. dollar amount
- Urgency words vs. benefit focus
- Question format vs. statement
- Local reference inclusion

### Offer Presentation
- Percentage vs. dollar savings
- List format vs. paragraph
- Multiple benefits vs. single focus
- Before/after pricing display

### Call-to-Action
- Button text variations ("Book Now" vs. "Claim Offer")
- Placement (top, middle, bottom)
- Color and size variations
- Multiple CTAs vs. single CTA

## Compliance & Best Practices

### Legal Requirements
- Clear terms and conditions
- Expiration date disclosure
- Service area limitations
- Pricing disclaimers

### Deliverability Best Practices
- Avoid spam trigger words
- Balance text and images
- Include plain text version
- Monitor sender reputation

### Customer Experience
- Mobile-optimized design
- Fast loading times
- Clear next steps
- Easy contact options

## Performance Metrics Targets

- **Open Rate:** 25-35% (promotional average)
- **Click Rate:** 4-8% (higher for promotions)
- **Conversion Rate:** 10-20% of clicks
- **Unsubscribe Rate:** <1%
- **Service Booking Rate:** 5-15% of opens

## Follow-Up Sequence

### Day 1: Initial Promotion Send
### Day 3: Reminder Email (non-openers)
### Day 7: Last Chance Email (non-clickers)
### Day 14: Different Offer (if applicable)

Each follow-up should have fresh subject lines and slightly different messaging while maintaining the core offer value.
