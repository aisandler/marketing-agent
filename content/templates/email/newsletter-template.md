## Content Guidelines

### Length Targets
- **Total Email:** 400-500 words
- **Subject Line:** 30-50 characters
- **Preview Text:** 35-90 characters

### Voice & Tone
- {{BRAND_VOICE}} - customer-centered approach
- Educational and helpful, not sales-heavy
- Professional but approachable
- Focus on customer benefits and peace of mind

### Mobile Optimization
- Single column layout
- Large, tappable CTA buttons
- Scannable with bullet points and short paragraphs
- Images optimized for mobile loading

### Compliance Requirements
- Clear unsubscribe link
- Physical address included
- CAN-SPAM compliant
- Privacy policy link
- Preference center option

## A/B Testing Elements

### Subject Lines
- Question vs. Statement format
- Benefit-focused vs. Educational
- With/without location references
- Emoji usage variations

### Content Variations
- Educational tip placement (top vs. middle)
- Customer story length (brief vs. detailed)
- CTA button text and placement
- Image vs. text-heavy sections

## Performance Metrics Targets

- **Open Rate:** 20-25% (industry average)
- **Click Rate:** 2-4% (industry average)
- **Unsubscribe Rate:** <0.5%
- **Forward Rate:** 1-2%
- **Service Inquiry Rate:** 5-10% of clicks

## Seasonal Adaptations

### Spring (March-May)
- Focus: Prevention and preparation
- Topics: Spring cleaning, seasonal service needs, property preparation

### Summer (June-August)
- Focus: Peak activity and immediate needs
- Topics: Active management, outdoor protection, vacation preparations

### Fall (September-November)
- Focus: Winter preparation and prevention
- Topics: Winterization, preventive services, pre-winter treatments

### Winter (December-February)
- Focus: Indoor comfort and planning
- Topics: Indoor maintenance, planning for spring, maintenance tips
