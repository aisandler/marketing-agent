# Negative Review Response Templates

## Template Structure: ACKNOWLEDGE → APOLOGIZE → ACT → ASSURE

### Google Review Response Template

#### For Service Quality Issues
```
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for taking the time to share your feedback. We sincerely apologize that our service didn't meet your expectations during your recent visit. This is not the standard of care we strive for at {{CLIENT_NAME}}.

We would very much like to make this right. Please contact me directly at {{PHONE_NUMBER}} or {{EMAIL_ADDRESS}} so we can discuss your experience and arrange a solution that ensures your complete satisfaction.

At {{CLIENT_NAME}}, we've been serving {{SERVICE_STATES}} families for {{YEARS_EXPERIENCE}} years, and customer satisfaction is our top priority. We appreciate the opportunity to earn back your trust.

Best regards,
[Manager Name]
{{CLIENT_NAME}}
```

#### For Scheduling/Communication Issues
```
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for your review, and I apologize for the scheduling confusion and poor communication you experienced. This is absolutely not the level of service we expect our team to provide.

Communication is crucial in our industry, and we clearly fell short of our standards. I would like to personally discuss this with you and ensure we have systems in place to prevent this from happening again.

Please call me directly at {{PHONE_NUMBER}} at your convenience. We value your feedback and want to make sure your experience with {{CLIENT_NAME}} reflects our {{YEARS_EXPERIENCE}} years of commitment to customer service excellence.

Sincerely,
[Manager Name]
{{CLIENT_NAME}}
```

## Response Customization Guidelines

### Tone Adjustments by Platform

#### Google Reviews (Professional but Personal)
- Use first person ("I apologize")
- Include manager/owner name
- Reference specific service details when appropriate
- Offer direct phone contact

#### Facebook (Conversational but Professional)
- Slightly more casual tone
- Use "we" more than "I"
- Encourage private message or phone call
- Show appreciation for feedback

#### Yelp (Balanced Professional)
- Professional but approachable
- Address specific concerns mentioned
- Offer multiple contact methods
- Reference business credentials

### Industry-Specific Modifications

#### Legal Services
```
Additional phrases to include:
- "Our clients' satisfaction is our top priority"
- "We stand behind our work"
- "Experienced, dedicated attorneys"
- "Comprehensive legal support"
```

#### Real Estate Services
```
Additional phrases to include:
- "Your success is our priority"
- "Market expertise and dedication"
- "Full-service support"
- "Transparent communication"
```

#### Professional Services
```
Additional phrases to include:
- "Excellence in every engagement"
- "Dedicated to your success"
- "Experienced professionals"
- "Client-focused approach"
```

## Escalation Triggers

### Immediate Management Alert Required
- Threats of legal action
- Mentions of property damage
- Safety or health concerns
- Media/reporter contact
- Multiple complaints about same issue

### Standard Follow-up Required
- First negative review in 6+ months
- Customer mentions competitors
- Service quality issues
- Pricing complaints
- Scheduling problems

## Response Time Requirements

### Critical Response (Within 1 Hour)
- 1-2 star reviews
- Safety/health concerns
- Angry social media posts
- Viral negative content

### Standard Response (Within 4 Hours)
- 3 star reviews with complaints
- General service issues
- Scheduling complaints
- Pricing concerns

### Follow-up Response (Within 24 Hours)
- 4-5 star reviews with minor issues
- Thank you responses
- Positive feedback acknowledgment

## Quality Checklist

Before sending any review response:
- [ ] Acknowledges specific customer concern
- [ ] Shows genuine empathy/apology
- [ ] Offers concrete next steps
- [ ] Includes direct contact information
- [ ] Maintains professional tone
- [ ] References company values/experience
- [ ] Avoids defensive language
- [ ] Doesn't share other customer info
- [ ] Stays within character limits
- [ ] Has been proofread for errors

## Follow-up Protocol

### After Public Response
1. Monitor for customer reply within 48 hours
2. Attempt private contact if no response
3. Document resolution attempts
4. Flag unresolved issues for management review

### Resolution Documentation
- Customer contact attempts made
- Resolution offered and accepted/declined
- Final outcome and customer satisfaction
- Lessons learned for process improvement

## Legal Considerations

### Never Include in Responses
- Admission of fault or liability
- Specific treatment methods or chemicals
- Other customer information
- Company financial information
- Employee personal information

### Always Include When Relevant
- Licensed and insured status
- Years of experience serving community
- Commitment to industry standards
- Willingness to work toward resolution
