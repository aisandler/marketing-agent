## Crisis Communication Protocols

### Media Contact Decision Tree

**Step 1: Initial Contact Assessment**
- Who is calling? (Reporter, blogger, TV station, etc.)
- What information are they seeking?
- What is their deadline?
- Have they already published anything?

**Step 2: Response Timeline**
- Immediate response: "I'll need to get back to you with that information"
- Internal consultation: Contact management/legal within 30 minutes
- Response preparation: Craft appropriate statement within 2 hours
- Follow-up: Deliver response within agreed timeline

**Step 3: Statement Approval Process**
- Draft statement using appropriate template
- Legal review if necessary
- Management approval required
- Designated spokesperson delivery only

### Social Media Monitoring Protocol

**Daily Monitoring:**
- Google alerts for company name
- Social media platform searches
- Review site monitoring
- Industry forum discussions

**Crisis Escalation Triggers:**
- Multiple negative posts within 24 hours
- Posts mentioning safety or legal issues
- Viral negative content (>100 shares/likes)
- Media outlet social media posts

**Response Timeline:**
- Acknowledge within 1 hour during business hours
- Full response within 4 hours
- Follow-up monitoring every 2 hours
- Management notification for all crisis-level activity

### Legal Consultation Requirements

**Immediate Legal Review Required:**
- Threats of litigation
- Safety or health claims
- Regulatory violation allegations
- Property damage claims
- Employee misconduct allegations

**Standard Legal Review Recommended:**
- Media inquiries about company practices
- Customer complaints mentioning legal action
- Industry-wide issue involvement
- Regulatory inquiry responses

### Recovery and Reputation Repair

**Short-term Actions (1-7 days):**
- Address immediate customer concerns
- Implement any necessary operational changes
- Monitor for additional issues or escalation
- Maintain consistent messaging across all channels

**Medium-term Actions (1-4 weeks):**
- Proactive positive content creation
- Customer satisfaction survey and feedback collection
- Community engagement and relationship building
- Staff training and process improvements

**Long-term Actions (1-6 months):**
- Reputation monitoring and improvement tracking
- Process evaluation and permanent improvements
- Relationship rebuilding with key stakeholders
- Prevention strategy implementation for future issues
