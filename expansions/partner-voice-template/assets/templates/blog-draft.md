## Template Notes

### Structure Guidance
- 800-1500 words is typical for blog posts
- Use subheads every 200-300 words
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Include at least one partner quote

### Voice Check
- [ ] Sounds like {{PARTNER_NAME}} (matches voice profile)
- [ ] Educational without being condescending
- [ ] Practical and actionable
- [ ] Demonstrates expertise through specifics

### Quality Filter
- [ ] Passes audience sophistication test throughout
- [ ] Doesn't explain basics
- [ ] Offers genuine insight at each section

### SEO Considerations
- [ ] Primary keyword in headline
- [ ] Keywords in subheads
- [ ] Meta description is compelling
- [ ] Internal linking opportunities noted
