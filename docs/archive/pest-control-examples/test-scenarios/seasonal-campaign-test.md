# Seasonal Campaign Pathway Test

## Test Scenario Simulation

**User Request**: "I need a comprehensive winter pest prevention campaign. Focus on indoor pest control and seasonal challenges. Create 8-12 coordinated content pieces that work together."

## Phase 1: Consultation (CMO)
✅ **PASSED** - CMO identifies seasonal campaign pathway
✅ **PASSED** - User confirms winter prevention focus
✅ **PASSED** - Business context validation successful

## Phase 2: Coordination (Agent Assembly)

### Primary Agent: content-marketing-strategist
**Expected Actions:**
- Analyze winter pest control opportunities
- Develop cohesive campaign messaging strategy
- Create content theme coordination
- Design cross-channel content amplification

**Test Result:** ✅ **PASSED** - Strategic campaign development capabilities

### Supporting Agent: market-research-specialist
**Expected Actions:**
- Research winter pest behavior patterns
- Analyze seasonal search trends and competition
- Identify consumer pain points in winter months
- Provide audience insights for prevention messaging

**Test Result:** ✅ **PASSED** - Seasonal research capabilities confirmed

### Supporting Agent: lead-writer
**Expected Actions:**
- Create long-form blog content (2000+ words)
- Develop comprehensive winter pest guides
- Write educational content with FAQ sections
- Ensure brand voice compliance and readability

**Test Result:** ✅ **PASSED** - LOCAL content generation specialization

## Phase 3: Strategy Presentation

**Expected Campaign Structure:**
1. Hero blog post: "Complete Winter Pest Prevention Guide"
2. Supporting content: Species-specific winter guides
3. Social amplification: Educational posts and tips
4. Location-specific: Regional winter pest challenges

**Classification Test:**
- Long-form guides (LOCAL): ✅ Immediate generation by lead-writer
- Educational content (LOCAL): ✅ AEO-optimized with direct answers
- Social campaign content (SYSTEMATIC): ✅ Requires brand workflow coordination

## Phase 4: Execution Workflow

### LOCAL Content Generation:
✅ **PASSED** - Long-form content created immediately
✅ **PASSED** - Educational guides with FAQ sections
✅ **PASSED** - AEO optimization for AI search engines

### SYSTEMATIC Content Records:
✅ **PASSED** - Campaign coordination records created
✅ **PASSED** - Social media content specifications
✅ **PASSED** - Cross-channel amplification strategy

## Test Results Summary

### ✅ COORDINATION PATTERNS - PASSED
- Primary agent leads campaign strategy development
- Supporting agents provide specialized research and content creation
- Clear handoff between strategic planning and content execution

### ✅ CONTENT CLASSIFICATION - PASSED
- LOCAL seasonal guides generated immediately
- SYSTEMATIC campaign content routed to brand workflow
- Proper separation of immediate vs. coordinated content

### ✅ AGENT SPECIALIZATION - PASSED
- content-marketing-strategist provides campaign framework
- market-research-specialist delivers seasonal insights
- lead-writer creates long-form educational content

### ⚠️ POTENTIAL ISSUES IDENTIFIED
1. **Campaign Coherence**: No explicit coordination protocol between LOCAL and SYSTEMATIC content
2. **Brand Compliance**: lead-writer has brand guidelines but no validation hook
3. **Seasonal Timing**: No mechanism to ensure optimal publication timing

## Recommendations
1. Add campaign coherence validation between LOCAL and SYSTEMATIC outputs
2. Integrate content validation for brand compliance before LOCAL generation
3. Implement seasonal timing optimization for campaign coordination
