## SEO Integration Strategy

### Primary Keyword Targeting
**Blog Posts:** Long-tail, educational keywords with local modifiers
**Social Media:** Engagement-focused with local hashtags
**Location Pages:** High-volume transactional keywords + geo modifiers

### Content-to-Keyword Mapping
- **"[service] attorney [city]"** → Location landing pages
- **"how to [legal topic] in [city]"** → Educational blog posts
- **"[service] lawyer near me"** → Social proof and urgency posts
- **"experienced [practice area] attorney"** → Authority and expertise content

## Content Quality Standards

### All Content Must Include:
- **Local expertise mentions** ("serving [area] for [timeframe]")
- **StoryBrand messaging** (empathy → authority → solution)
- **Clear CTAs** (phone number + specific action)
- **Family safety emphasis** ("protect what matters most")
- **Guarantee mentions** ("full-service guarantee")

### Technical Requirements:
- **WordPress-ready formatting** (headers, bullets, image placeholders)
- **Mobile-optimized structure** (short paragraphs, scannable)
- **Local SEO elements** (NAP, location keywords, schema markup)
- **Internal linking** (pest types → services → locations)
